The Race

Waukesha County Circuit Court. Paul is running to bring experience, fairness, and integrity to the bench.

Judicial Philosophy

What Kind of Judge Will Paul Be?

Paul believes the best judges lead with firmness, intelligence, fairness, and kindness. They are servant leaders — approachable, accessible, and committed to making the courtroom a place where justice is available to all.

With experience as both a defense attorney and prosecutor, Paul understands the justice system from every perspective. He has presided over hundreds of cases and knows that every person who enters a courtroom deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
