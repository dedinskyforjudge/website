Judicial Philosophy

What Kind of Judge Will Paul Be?

Paul believes the best judges lead with firmness, intelligence, and fairness. They are public servants — approachable, accessible, and committed to making the courtroom a place where justice is available to all.

Paul understands the justice system from every perspective. He has presided over hundreds of cases and knows that every person who enters a courtroom deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
