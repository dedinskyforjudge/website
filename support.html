Ways You Can Support Paul

Display a Yard Sign

Put a Dedinsky for Judge sign in your yard and let your neighbors know who you're voting for. Yard signs are one of the highest-impact, lowest-effort ways to support a local campaign.

Host a Fundraiser

Open your home — or a local venue — to introduce Paul to friends, neighbors, and colleagues. Even a small gathering helps build the financial foundation a winning campaign needs.

Knock on Doors

Door-to-door conversations are how judicial races are won. Join the canvassing team and help spread the word in your neighborhood and across Waukesha County.

Host a Meet & Greet

Invite Paul to a casual gathering of neighbors and friends. These intimate conversations are where voters meet the person behind the campaign and decide who they trust.
