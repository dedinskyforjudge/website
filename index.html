About Paul

A Career Dedicated to Justice

Paul Dedinsky has spent his career in public service — as a criminal defense attorney, a prosecutor, a chief legal counsel, and a circuit court judge. Over more than 25 years in the law, he has tried over 50 jury trials and 100 court trials, authored Wisconsin's leading domestic violence prosecution manual, and trained thousands of educators and students in restorative justice.

Born and raised in Milwaukee, Paul earned his J.D. from the University of Wisconsin–Madison and his Ph.D. in Education & Leadership from Cardinal Stritch University. He and his wife Lisa have three children.
